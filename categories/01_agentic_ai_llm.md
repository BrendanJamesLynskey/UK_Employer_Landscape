# Category 01 — Agentic AI / LLM Engineering

> Python · LangGraph · LangChain · MCP · Ollama · multi-agent · open-weights LLMs · TypeScript

## Summary

The UK has one of the densest agentic-AI / LLM engineering ecosystems outside the US, anchored by **London** (DeepMind, Anthropic, OpenAI, Cohere, Mistral, Synthesia, Wayve, PolyAI, ElevenLabs, Stability) and **Cambridge** (Microsoft Research, Speechmatics, Featurespace, Darktrace, Arm AI). Yorkshire and Greater Manchester host a smaller but real cluster — notably **Peak (Manchester)**, **Sky Leeds**, **AutoTrader**, **Lloyds**, and **Co-op** data-platform teams — where a hybrid HW + AI bridge profile is unusually well-matched. As of April 2026, hiring is strong: London dominates with ~1,500–2,500 open agent/LLM roles; Manchester + Leeds together carry low-hundreds of relevant postings; UK-remote roles at Hugging Face, Anthropic, Mistral, Cohere, Faculty and Quantexa are realistically high-leverage.

## Yorkshire & Greater Manchester focus

**Greater Manchester** is the strongest regional cluster after London/Cambridge for applied LLM work. **Peak** (NOMA) openly uses LangChain/LangGraph patterns and is productising agentic decision-intelligence. **AutoTrader** has a sizeable in-house ML platform team and is shipping GenAI features. **Co-op Digital** (Angel Square) and **Lloyds Banking Group** Manchester each have GenAI/agent platform teams (LLM gateway, internal RAG copilots). **AND Digital** and **BJSS** (now CGI) operate from Manchester and Leeds and run real GenAI client engagements. **Cisco AppDynamics** (Manchester) is integrating LLM-driven observability agents. **BBC R&D** maintains a North Lab in Salford.

**Yorkshire**: **Sky's Leeds tech hub** (Leeds Dock) is the largest single AI/data engineering employer in the region — they run a GenAI platform team and have shipped LLM-backed customer-care agents. **Infinity Works** (now IBM Consulting), **Channel 4** (Majestic Building, Leeds), **Asda** (Leeds HQ), **NHS England** AI Lab (Leeds), and **Rockstar Leeds** all have real GenAI engagements. The Yorkshire cluster is smaller (~60–120 live LLM/agent postings vs Manchester's ~150–250) but a hybrid HW/FPGA + agentic-AI background is rare locally and stands out.

For UK-remote roles, **Hugging Face**, **Anthropic**, **Mistral**, **Cohere**, **Faculty**, and **Quantexa** all hire Northern-England-remote engineers — realistically the highest-leverage targets for an open-weights / LangGraph / MCP focus.

## Notes & Caveats

- Headcounts and posting counts are order-of-magnitude estimates from LinkedIn Jobs and company careers pages, late April 2026; LinkedIn's "agent" keyword is noisy (matches insurance, real-estate, customer-service "agent" roles).
- "Hiring?" reflects whether at least one relevant senior IC role was visible in April 2026; this changes weekly.
- Anthropic UK, OpenAI UK, Mistral London have small UK headcounts (<50) but very high relevance — competition is intense.
